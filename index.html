Reserach Experiences

    In 2016-2017, I joined the Laboratory for Future Networks(LFN), directed by Prof. Feng Wu, and then worked in a group led by Prof. Cong Shen, mainly doing research in Reinforcement Learning and Network. I joined a Reinforcement Learning Group especially focused on Multi-armed Bandit(MAB) problem, and my research topic is "Multi-armed Bandit in Wireless Networks". I also joined in a project focused on Demand-side Management and Energy Consumption Scheduling with Reinforcement Learning and Game Framework.

    In 2017-2018, I joined Visual Computing Group at MSRA and was focused on CNN architecture design and optimization problem in training ultra deep neural networks. We proposed a novel network architecture called "Merge-and-Run" network, which achieves state-of-the-art performance. We also re-studied the residual connection in ResNets and conduct a novel analysis of residual network architecture. My mentor is Dr. Jingdong Wang who gave me a lot of useful advises in research.
